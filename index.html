Official Government Alternatives

If you prefer official, government-backed online training and certification programs rather than physical classes, consider these highly recommended national initiatives.

DGFT Learn Portal

Direct access to official Foreign Trade Policy (FTP) handbooks, registration guidelines, and helpdesk tools. Entirely free knowledge repository.

IIFT Niryat Bandhu

An official Ministry of Commerce initiative run by the Indian Institute of Foreign Trade. Official online certification programs.
